Final Year Project (Ongoing)

Statistical Analysis of Sunspot Properties using SDO/HMI Data

Role: Student Researcher

Focus: Solar Physics & Computational Astronomy

I am currently conducting extensive research to determine the statistical distributions of sunspot sizes and temperatures, utilizing a massive dataset from the Solar Dynamics Observatory (SDO).

Key Research Methodologies:

  • Big Data Handling: Analyzing a sample of approximately 4,000 sunspots observed between 2010 and 2026, integrating catalog data from Solar Monitor with high-resolution HMI continuum observations.
  • Computational Techniques: Implementing feature-tracking algorithms and solar differential rotation laws to precisely time central meridian crossings, minimizing projection effects.
  • Image Processing: Applying advanced image segmentation to isolate sunspot umbra and penumbra regions for accurate area measurement.
  • Thermal Analysis: Estimating sunspot temperatures via blackbody approximation of observed continuum intensities to constrain thermal properties of solar magnetic activity.
